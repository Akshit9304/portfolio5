My Work

Sign Language Recognition Using Python and Open CV

In this sign language recognition project, we created a sign detector, which detects numbers from 1 to 10 that can very easily be extended to cover a vast multitude of other signs and hand gestures including the alphabets. We have developed this project using OpenCV and Keras modules of python.

Cell Phone detector using CA3130 Op-Amp

Cell Phone Detector is a circuit that detects the presence of Cell phone up to a certain range. This circuit detects the phone when an incoming call or outgoing call is made or when an SMS is sent or received. It can also be called as Frequency Detector. When the circuit detects an RF signal from an activated Cell phone, it gives an indication by switching ON an LED.
